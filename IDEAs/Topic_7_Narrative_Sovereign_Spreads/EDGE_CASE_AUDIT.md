# Edge case audit

## Pricing

The contraction condition is sufficient for a unique within-period spread. Failure of that condition does not prove multiplicity. The direct belief gap is always single-peaked. Equilibrium single-peakedness additionally requires the derivative restriction in Proposition 1. The code evaluates its analytic margin over the reported state space.

## Time aggregation

Monthly persistence, innovation variance, and spread feedback aggregate exactly to their annual counterparts. The test suite verifies all identities to numerical precision.

## Prevalence

The continuous-time logistic vector field leaves the unit interval invariant. Monthly simulation uses the exact flow under rates frozen for that month. It does not rely on truncation to keep prevalence feasible.

## Global dynamics

An instantaneous zero-drift root is not a global basin boundary. The paper proves only its local movement. Distinct seeded and unseeded outcomes require invariant-set conditions and are reported as a scenario path supported by a phase portrait.

## Measurement and policy

Media diffusion is not investor transmission. The state-space measurement equation separates observed text from latent prevalence. A binding spread cap removes the marginal narrative effect only for the modeled instrument. OMT is treated as a compound policy event.
