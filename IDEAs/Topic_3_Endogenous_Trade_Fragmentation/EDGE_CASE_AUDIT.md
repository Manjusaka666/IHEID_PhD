# Edge-case audit

- Setting `theta = xi = chi = eta_a = 0` removes strategic complementarity.
- The capacity denominator requires `1 - chi F_m > 0` on the unit interval.
- A best-response slope above one does not prove a fold.
- Each reported fold solves the equilibrium and tangency equations with nonzero curvature and transversality derivatives.
- The scalar multiplier is used only for a decoupled sector.
- The full system reports `(I - J)^{-1}` and requires spectral radius below one for local stability.
- Partial-adjustment hysteresis is separated from forward-looking equilibrium selection and physical irreversibility.
- The maintenance subsidy is positive only when the independence value is below the model wedge.
- Fold distances and policy wedges are scenario outputs until the feedback parameters are jointly estimated.
- BACI relationships are product-country links and are never described as observed firm-supplier links.
